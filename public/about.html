About QuickTools

QuickTools is a free online platform created to help students and professionals improve productivity, prepare for placements, and simplify daily tasks.

Our platform combines multiple tools in one place — including aptitude practice, resume building, AI-powered interview preparation, and useful calculators.

🚀 What We Offer

  • ✔ Aptitude Practice Questions for placements
  • ✔ AI Interview Practice (Aptitude, Python, Java)
  • ✔ Resume Builder & ATS Checker
  • ✔ Productivity Tools & Calculators

🎯 Our Mission

Our mission is to make placement preparation easy and accessible for everyone by providing simple, fast, and free tools.
